## Leadership and Impact

Currently, I serve as the **VP of Data at Shopify**, where I drive the strategy for analytics, data engineering, and data science to power merchant success and product innovation.

Prior to this, I served as the **Chief Technology Officer at the Democratic National Committee (DNC)**, where I led the team responsible for [cybersecurity](/talks#active-measures-disinformation-campaigns-and-election-security), [product development](/talks#dncs-new-tech-leader-talks-about-what-went-wrong-in-2016-and-how-dems-are-preparing-for-2020), and the [data infrastructure](/talks#data-should-enfranchise-people-says-the-democrats-head-of-technology) used to support democratic campaigns nationwide. My background also includes leadership roles at **Facebook**, where I led data science teams for the advertising ecosystem, and **Hillary for America**, where I served as Deputy Chief Analytics Officer.
